About

Hello, my name is Kaden. I am a web developer currently residing in Vancouver, British Columbia. I am offering my web development and design skills to be able to study full-time to become a software engineer.

As far as education goes, due to financial reasons, I have had to teach myself web development online using websites such as Udemy. I have taken several courses including but not limited to: HTML, CSS, Git, Vanilla Javascript, JQuery, NodeJS, AJAX, D3 and SVG; React and JSX; and Ruby on Rails.

With my experience I am well-versed and confident with front-end development and continually working towards improving my abilities with databases, asynchronous code, and general object oriented programming so that I can become a skilled full-stack developer.

My goal is to develop an extremely great understanding of many forms of programming, everywhere from blockchain programming to artificial intelligence. Ideally, my career focus would be directing production of artificial intelligence in a safe, ethical and positive direction - much like an idol of mine, Elon Musk.
